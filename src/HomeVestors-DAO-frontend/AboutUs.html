What Makes HomeVestors DAO Different?

Each property on our platform is structured as a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization).
Membership is represented by NFTs, not as ownership, but as your key to participating in governance decisions for that specific property.

Decisions like maintenance, rent distribution, and upgrades are voted on by DAO members. Participation in governance is essential, and active members shape the future of the properties they help manage.
