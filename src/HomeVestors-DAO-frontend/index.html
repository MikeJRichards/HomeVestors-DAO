What Is HomeVestors DAO?

HomeVestors DAO brings real-world property onto the blockchain making participation faster, more flexible, and accessible to all.

Each property on our platform is structured as a DAO, governed by its NFT-holding members. These NFTs represent membership and voting rights and ownership thorough code and are instantly tradable. Rental rewards are distributed proportional to holdings, to all that participate in governance ensuring fairness, transparency, and community control.

How It Works

1. Choose a Property DAO

Browse listed properties. Each has its own DAO, tenants, and governance process.

2. Join via NFT Membership

Purchase an NFT to become a DAO member. Its quick, low-cost, and on-chain.

3. Participate in Governance

Vote on decisions such as maintenance, budgets, and rent distribution.

4. Exit or Unlock Flexibility

NFTs can be resold or exited with a built-in liquidity mechanism.
In future, you'll also be able to unlock liquidity without selling by minting stablecoins against your NFTs.

About Us

Reimagining Property Participation for Web3

HomeVestors DAO was born out of frustration the kind you only know if you've ever dealt with mortgages, repairs, void periods, and slow systems. As landlords, we've dealt with the inefficiencies, gatekeeping, and paperwork that define traditional property systems.

We knew there had to be a better way one where people could participate meaningfully in real-world assets through transparent, collective decision-making.

So we built it.

Our Mission

To make real estate participation more open, accessible, and governed by the people who engage with it.

Our Vision

A future where properties are governed by active communities using decentralized tools not held behind layers of bureaucracy or exclusive capital and powered by unstoppable Web3 infrastructure like the Internet Computer.

What Makes HomeVestors DAO Different?

Each property on our platform is structured as a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization).
Membership is represented by NFTs as your key to participating in governance decisions for that specific property.

Decisions like maintenance, rent distribution, and upgrades are voted on by DAO members. Participation in governance is essential, and active members shape the future of the properties they help manage.
